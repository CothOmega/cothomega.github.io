Abstract Sequential

I have known I was an Abstract Sequential thinker for some time. Back in high school, I took a career test before accepting my college acceptance. I wanted to know that I mentally fit into where I was planning to be. Sadly, that didn't exactly work out as planned in that instance, but it has definitely helped give me perspective ocer the years.

So far, my style has lended my some benefits to the way Phase 0 is layed out. Two of the bigger aspects I play to often include heavily relying on reference material and my success when working alone. But we can already see where that would cause issue. I am a very strong introvert, and the pairing process has definitely taken me out of my comfort zone. I totally recognize the benefits of the task, but that doesn't make it easy for me. I have actually even rescheduled some of my pair groups due to sheer anxiety over working intimately with other people. I always overcome it, but it can be trying.

Introspection is a good tool to have at your disposal. Focusing on my strengths and shoring up my weaknesses will help ensure DBC is successful for me.
