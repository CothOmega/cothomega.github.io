Why JavaScript?

JavaScript is widely used for various reasons. It has uses in client-side interaction as well as server-side development. It has also risen to be a major card in mobile app development. And when mixed with AJAX, we gain lots of ability in manipulating DOM elements and making database calls.

JS can have some downsides, though. From things I have read, it doesn't seem to excel at large databases or huge enterprise apps. I honestly don't know enough from personal interaction, but I found more than one internet flame war of people arguing: one side that JS was the future of all coding, the other arguing that JS doesn't even qualify as a "good" programming language.
