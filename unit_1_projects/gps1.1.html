About Pair Programming

  • Pair programming is important because being able to have immediate feedback helps design or creative processes.
  • Correcting errors.
  • Providing a different outlook
  • Learning from peers.
  • Ability to learn or teach your peer.

Driving Vs. Navigating

Navigators main job is to keep the job conceptually on task. They need to have a big picture view of the project.this allows them to properly issue task to the driver.

Drivers main job is putting the code to work. They also gets the hands on and get to code.
